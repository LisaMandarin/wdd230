Chamber Luncheon

Our monthly luncheon sits community leaders, business owners, nonprofit executives and staffers at the same table, and enables us to reconnect, engage and learn from each other.

Our goal is to provide insight into business, community and legislative issues and share the stories of those making a difference. We want audience members to leave with ideas and information about how to better their personal and professional lives.

Every in-person luncheon provides a great opportunity for you to network with other Kaohsiung businesses while also enjoying a great locally catered meal. Each month we seek to have an engaging presentation available for you, your business, staff and colleagues.
