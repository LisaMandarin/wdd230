Site Plan

Kaohsiung Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

This website provides valuable insights into the historical background and organizational structure of the Kaohsiung Chamber of Commerce. Website visitors can peruse a comprehensive list of member companies and even take the initiative to join this esteemed community. It also serves as a hub for government commercial policy updates and offers convenient registration for a diverse range of business training courses. Visitors can engage in vibrant networking events with fellow members, and explore detailed information regarding venue rentals.

Scenarios

  • What requirement should an organization or a pereson meet to join the Kaohsiung Chamber of Commerce?
  • What network events will the chamber be holding this month?
  • What business training courses are available in the upcoming three months?
